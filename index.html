How to Play

  • Goal: Capture white pieces to score points. Survive as long as you can.
  • Your pieces are black. Drag and drop them to move.
  • Piece values: Pawn 1, Knight 2.8, Bishop 3.2, Rook 4.8, King 4, Queen 9.3.
  • Convertable pieces (marked with a ★) join your side when captured.
  • Promotion: Hold Shift when moving a pawn to the last rank to choose a piece.
  • Clear the board of all white pieces for a +100 bonus!
  • Game Over when you have no legal moves left.
