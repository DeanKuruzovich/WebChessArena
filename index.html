How to Play

  • Goal: Capture white pieces for points and survive as long as you can.
  • Controls: Drag and drop your pieces to move.
  • Turns: You move, then white moves. After that, new white pieces may appear.
  • Forming pieces: Newly spawned pieces are “forming” for a few turns and can’t move yet.
  • Scoring: Captures are worth points based on the piece you take (bigger pieces are worth more).
  • Speed bonus: Faster moves give a small bonus each turn.
  • Promotion: Reach the last rank with a pawn to promote (hold Shift while moving to choose the piece, if not Queen automaticly chosen).
  • ★ Convertible pieces: Capturing a ★ piece adds that piece to your side later (it spawns in and may start forming).
  • Game Over: When you have no legal moves left (or no pieces).
  • CM title: 10,000 lifetime points or 500 best score.
  • FM title: 25,000 lifetime points or 1,000 best score.
  • IM title: 50,000 lifetime points or 3,000 best score.
  • GM title: 100,000 lifetime points or 10,000 best score.
